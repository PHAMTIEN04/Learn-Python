
**Mouse Operations:**

**ActionChains:**
1. **Mouse Hover**
   - Syntax: `move_to_element(element)`
2. **Right Click**
   - Syntax: `context_click(element)`
3. **Double Click**
   - Syntax: `double_click(element)`
4. **Drag and Drop**
   - Syntax: `drag_and_drop(source, target)`

**Slider:**
- Syntax: `drag_and_drop_by_offset(element, X, Y)`

**Scrolling Page:**
- Syntax: `driver.execute_script("window.scrollBy(x, y)")`
- Syntax: `driver.execute_script("return window.pageYOffset;")` (returns the scroll value for Y-axis)
- Syntax: `driver.execute_script("arguments[0].scrollIntoView();", flag)` (scroll until the element is in view)
- Syntax: `driver.execute_script("window.scrollBy(0, document.body.scrollHeight)")` (scroll to the bottom of the page)
- Syntax: `driver.execute_script("window.scrollBy(0, -document.body.scrollHeight)")` (scroll to the top of the page)
